Before you file

If you haven't already, we recommend to sign up for My Business Account (MyBA) as early as possible to file your return online.

If you have never used our digital services before, go to My Business Account for charitites for information on how to create an account and start filing your T3010 Return.

Check to make sure your return is complete

When filing your T3010 Return, make sure your contact information is still valid. This allows the public and the CRA to communicate with your charity.

A complete return must include the following forms and documents:

  • Form T3010, Registered Charity Information Return
    • Section C2: Describe your charitable activites, not your fundraising activities
    • Section E: Include the signature of a director, trustee, or like official
  • Form T1235, Directors/Trustees and Like Officials Worksheet
    • Include all the information requested for each director, trustee, and like official
    • If your directors, trustees, or like officials have not indicated their position, you can leave the "Term-End date" field blank
  • Financial statements (even if your charity was inactive)
    • Must all have the same fiscal year-end
  • Form T1236, Qualified donees worksheet / Amounts provided to other organizations (if applicable)
    • Include the registration numbers for all qualified donees
  • Form T2081, Excess corporate holding worksheet for private foundations (if applicable)
Additional details for filing a paper return

Make sure your financial information is correct

In the T3010 Return, you must include your financial information in either Section D or Schedule 6 - do not complete both.

You must complete Schedule 6 (not Section D) if any of the following applies:

  • The charity's revenue exceeds $100,000
  • The amount of all property (for example, investments, rental properties) not used in charitable activities is more than $25,000
  • The charity has permission to accumulate funds during this fiscal year

If you are completing Section D:

  • Lines 4500 to 4650 (excluding line 4505) must equal the amount on line 4700
  • Lines 4860 to 4920 must equal the amount on line 4950
  • Line 5000 must show the total amount spent on charitable programs

If you are completing Schedule 6:

  • Lines 4500, 4510 to 4580, and 4600 to 4650 must equal the amount on line 4700
  • Lines 4800 to 4920 must equal the amount on line 4950
  • Only enter amounts on lines 5500 and 5510 if you have received permission to accumulate funds
  • Enter amounts on lines 5900 and 5910 if the charity has property that was not used in charitable activities
  • Line 5000 must show the total amount spent on charitable programs

When to file

You must file a complete T3010 Return no later than 6 months after the end of your fiscal period.
