Filing a Registered Charity Information Return (T3010)

All registered charities must file a complete T3010 Registered Charity Information Return annually to maintain charitable registration, and to provide the CRA with the required information to remain compliant.

Filing your return also provides transparency for donors who want accurate and up-to-date information about registered charities.
