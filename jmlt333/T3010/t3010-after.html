After you file

After you file your T3010 Return, we will send you a confirmation of annual information return filing.

The information on your return will be available on the List of charities. You are responsible for making sure that your return is correct. If you notice an error, make sure to amend your return. Do not file another return for the same year.

If your charity received approval for a disbursement quota reduction

If you received approval for a disbursement quota reduction, you must also file Form T1240, Registered Charity Adjustment Request, to correct the return for the fiscal year the shortfall occurred in.

Amending a return after you have filed

Online:

Sign in to My Business Account (MyBA) and select "Adjust a return".

Sign in to My Business Account Register

Refer to the hours for online services.

By mail or fax:
Complete an adjustment request
  1. Complete Form T1240 Registered Charity Adjustment Request for each T3010 Return that you need to correct
  2. Enter the fiscal year-end on each form
  3. The form must be signed by a representative of your charity that the Charities Directorate has on file
  4. Mail or fax your amended return

Complete an adjustment request for other forms filed with your return
  1. Complete Form T1240 Registered Charity Adjustment Request and attach the amended form (for example, Form T1235 or Form T1236)
  2. Keep copies of Form T1240 and any revised forms with your books and records
  3. The form must be signed by a representative of your charity that the Charities Directorate has on file
  4. Mail or fax your amended return
