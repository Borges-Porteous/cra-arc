Takeaway points

Steps to follow

  1. Review their background information and required slips
  2. Click T4 and employment income in the left-side menu
  3. Click the + sign next to T4 income (earned in any province except Quebec) in the T4 and T4E section
  4. Enter the Employer’s name and the amounts from the individual’s T4 slip into the corresponding fields

Entering the employers name is not mandatory but helps you to keep track in cases where there are multiple slips.

If box 24 or box 26 of a T4 slip are blank, do not enter 0 in in the tax software; leave the fields blank.
