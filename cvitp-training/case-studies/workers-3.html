Takeaway points

Steps to follow

  1. Review their background information and required slips
  2. Click T4 and employment income in the left-side menu
  3. Click the + sign next to T4 income (earned in any province except Quebec) in the T4 and T4E section and enter the Employer’s name and the amounts from the individual’s T4 slips into the corresponding fields
  4. Click T4 and employment income in the left-side menu and click the + sign next to Tips in the Other section to enter the total for tips and gratuities received during the year that are not included on their T4 slip
  5. Click T4 and employment income in the left-side menu and click the + sign next to CPT20 – CPP Election for Other employment earnings in the CPT20 section
  6. Select B Employment received tips, gratuities, or other earnings from the Select the type of employment on which you elect to pay CPP contributions (CPT20) drop-down menu
  7. Enter the following, in order, into each of the Other employment income, enter the gross amount earned fields:
    1. type of income (In this case, Tips)
    2. gross amount earned that the individual wants to make additional CPP contributions for
    3. address of the employer

The tax software automatically calculates the additional CPP contributions applicable to the other employment income.
