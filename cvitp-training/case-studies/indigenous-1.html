Takeaway points

Steps to follow

  1. Review their background information and required slips
  2.  In Interview setup, tick the box next to Tax return for a Status Indian in the Specific situations section and tick the box next to Employment income and employment insurance benefits (T4, T4E/RL-6) in the Employment and other benefits section
  3. Click Status Indian in the left-side menu and select Yes from the Please confirm that you are a registered Indian, or entitled to be registered as an Indian under the Indian Act drop-down menu
  4. Click T4 and employment income in the left-side menu and click the + sign next to T4 income (earned in any province except Quebec)
  5. Complete the necessary fields
  6. Select Exempt CPP/QPP (rare!) from the field 28. Box 71 – Indian (exempt income) – Employment drop-down menu in the Other information section

Even though Christie lives off reserve, her income is tax exempt because it is earned on a reserve and she has Indian status. The software automatically generates form T90 which allows Christie to include this income in the calculation of the Canada workers benefit (CWB).
